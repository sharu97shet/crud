Career

It is matter of pride for us to state that all those who started their careers in our organization either as trainees or audit assistants are doing extremely well, with some of them heading their respective departments in reputed companies. The following placements are available.

  • Articled / Audit Trainees

    This is a compulsory internship required to be undergone by every person desiring to become a Chartered Accountant. The period of training is normally for Three years, during which the student simultaneously appears for the examinations conducted by the ICAI. We offer hands-on exposure to the student in all aspects of Auditing, Taxation and Corporate laws and financial management under the guidance and supervision of seniors and the partners. Minimum qualification is a pass in the First Group of IPCC Examination conducted by the ICAI.

  • Audit & Accounts Executives And Admin Staff

    This is a pure employment opportunity for those with or without prior experience. Minimum desired qualification would be graduation in Commerce stream.

  • Professional Assignments

    We also maintain a panel of professionals and experienced persons to whom specific assignments are given on a case-to-case basis. Such assignments normally include handling audits, taxation and company law matters in courts and tribunals, giving legal opinions etc. Services of freshly qualified Chartered Accountants, Advocates, Cost Accountants, Company Secretaries and those who have completed their Articleship and have cleared their CA examinations partly are made use of.
